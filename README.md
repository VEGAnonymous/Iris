# **Mareverb**

![Mareverb GUI](https://u.pone.rs/qmfftudr.PNG "Mareverb GUI")

🎵 *"Mares in my mind"* 🦄

**Mareverb** is a dynamic convolution reverb implementing [TVOLAP (Time-Variant Overlap-Add in Partitions)](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2310.00319 "TVOLAP paper") to allow simultaneous convolution with up to 8 impulse responses, each weighted by their proximity to (You) in a representative *mare map*. The closer you are, the stronger their voice!

> **Features**
- **Mares!**
- Explore the space with a variety of motion patterns for both (You) and the amres
- Load random IRs from a directory list with filtering so you can overdose on your waifu's voice as much as you'd like
- Enjoy automatic mare detection, designed with Clipper's master files in mind *(highly recommended)*
- Use our patented "auto-swap" technology to allow new mares to surprise you at random intervals
